Rainbow Six Siege

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is the thirteenth installment in the Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Franchise

developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft Entertainment. it is the latest game to be released since

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Vegas 2 and is considered a successor to the now-canceled Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Patriots.
