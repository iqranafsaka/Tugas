Monster Hunter: World

Monster Hunter: World[a] is an action role-playing game developed and published by Capcom.

A part of the Monster Hunter series, it was released worldwide for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in January 2018,

with a Microsoft Windows version being scheduled for August 2018.
